featured swap: Gel Pen ATC

Jul 16, 2012 • rachel

I love gel pens! They are so fun to work with and look great on dark paper… so, this week I am featuring the Gel Pen ATC Swap hosted by PixelPixie.

ATC stands for Artist Trading Card. ATCs are 2.5″ x 3.5″ in size (the size of a usual playing card) and they are usually made of card stock. They can be hand drawn, decorated, and embellished in any way you like.

For this international swap, you must use gel pens in your ATC in some way. You have just one partner to whom you send just one ATC. Sign up before July 20th!
