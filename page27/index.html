Tuesday Tip: Holiday Shipping Dates

Nov 22, 2011 • rachel

The USPS has released their Holiday Shipping Info. Here are some of the important recommended First Class Mail shipping dates if you want to get items to your recipient by December 25:

**Domestic Mail – Ship by December 20th

International Mail, Africa & South America – Ship by December 2nd

International Mail, everywhere else – Ship by December 9th

**

Also:

  • Canada Post suggested holiday mailing dates.
  • Royal Mail recommended last posting dates for holiday packages.
steroids.com

The postal services work hard over the holidays to deliver a huge quantity of gifts and greeting cards, but keep in mind that items may be delayed. If possible, work in a few extra mail transit days into your swap planning. If you are creating a new swap, keep in mind that most post offices will be closed on December 25 and January 1st, so plan your mail deadlines accordingly.

There is still time to get in some fun holiday swapping, but don’t wait too long!!
