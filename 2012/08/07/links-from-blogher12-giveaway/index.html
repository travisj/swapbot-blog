In an attempt to spread the good word about Swap-bot and to learn more about running an online business, I sometimes attend conferences. You may remember my posts about Blogher in 2010 and BlogHer Handmade last year. Last week, I was at BlogHer 2012 right here in New York City, and I picked up a few links for you:

  • Martha Stewart was one of the keynote speakers. She was as arrogant confident as ever, but she was also really funny. She is definitely a hard worker and I think she might be a genius. Anyhoo, she has a new show, Martha Stewart’s Cooking School, starting in October on PBS. It looks very lovely.
  • Not a blog, but maybe a helpful tool: RescueTime. Track how you spend your time online and be more productive.
