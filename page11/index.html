You already know I am a crazy lady when it comes to Valentine’s Day! I just can’t help it. I love all things pink & red & glittery & lovey dovey. I know not everyone feels the same way …which is why this week’s featured swap encompasses a bit more than just the holiday of love.

Even if you aren’t a huge Valentine fan, come join my February Love Matchbox Swap!! Your matchbox does not necessarily have to be Valentine’s Day related. It can be of any February theme, like winter, President’s Day, MLK Jr Day, my birthday!! haha! The swap is totally sender’s choice, so you can get started on your February crafting now. (You can download a free, printable matchbox template HERE.)

This is the sixth edition of this international paper crafting swap and I hope we can get a huge group of participants. For the swap, you simply decorate and fill one matchbox for one partner and get it in the mail by February 7th. New members and experienced members with a 4.9 rating, or higher, are welcome to join. featured swap: Holiday in a Matchbox

Nov 25, 2013 • rachel

I always join a few matchbox swaps during the holiday season. The little, decorated boxes are like fun mini holiday gifts to make and receive!

This year, I am signed up for KarlaKC’s Christmas/Holiday in a Matchbox Swap. For the swap, you make at least one holiday-themed matchbox for your partner. You can use my free template to make your boxes, or simply decorate a real matchbox. Feel free get creative! Some crafters make a set of matchbox drawers by wrapping a few matchboxes together in a stack. You can fill your matchbox with goodies or create a shrine or diorama inside.

Check out some examples here.

The Christmas/Holiday in a Matchbox Swap is international. However, there is also a USA-only version. Each swap is open to users who have completed at least ten Type 3 swaps. Sign up by November 30th!!
