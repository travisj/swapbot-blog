More from the 100,000th Swap!

I have been compiling this blog post for far too long, but there are just soooo many awesome Swap-bot-themed crafts to share with you from the 100,000th Swap! Here are a few more of the wonderful Ernie incarnations…

So many different types of crafts are represented! These cute cupcake toppers were made by lorepuckett.

These awesome Ernie goodies were all handmade by arkaya.

This cute little pillow was sent to babytreese from effycrush.

Cool Swap-bot buttons made by bauerdesigns.

How cute is this mini, beaded Ernie charm created by BerlinGeorgia?!

This marvelous zippered pouch and address labels were made by sunnyblueskies for her partner.

Cakers made there personalized swap cards for her partner, LilyM.

Ernie, Crusher, and Hello Kitty all play together in cherriesberries’ handmade card.

And last but not least, I made these little, felt, Ernie business card holders. They didn’t turn out exactly as I pictured them, but I filled them with lots of Swap-bot stickers and cards.
